About me

My name is Zoheir Ziani (Double ℤ), and I am currently a physics lecturer at the University of Lille, where I teach general physics to a diverse group of students. While my current role is centered on teaching, I have a strong background in nonlinear optics and photonics, with previous research interests in modulation instability in Kerr media and Fabry-Perot resonators. Although I am not actively engaged in research at the moment, I remain deeply interested in the field and aspire to eventually pursue a professorship that would allow me to combine high-level teaching with meaningful contributions to scientific research.

Outside of academia, I'm a fan of strategy games and passionate about visual arts. These interests nurture my creativity and contribute to a balanced and fulfilling life, enriching both my personal growth and professional perspective.
